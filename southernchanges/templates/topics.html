Topics

The categories below were generated by topic modeling, a form a text mining that identifies clusters of related words across a body of texts. Topic modelling toolkit MALLET uses probability statistics to produce two kinds of data: the weighted frequencies of words within a grouping and the prominence of each grouping within each text. Roughly put, it creates word clouds that can be interpreted as “topics” and reveals which documents best represent each topic.
